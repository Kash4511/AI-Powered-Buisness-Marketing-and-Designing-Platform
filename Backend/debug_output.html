Core Principles of Sustainable Design

These principles work together to create buildings that perform better operationally while reducing long-term liabilities and maintenance costs for property owners and government entities.

Industry-standard metrics include Energy Use Intensity (EUI), LEED certification levels, and net-zero or near-zero energy targets. Architects must design buildings that meet or exceed these benchmarks while communicating performance expectations clearly to all stakeholders from project inception.
